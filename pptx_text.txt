PROBLEM STATEMENT
How might we utilize AI chatbots and machine learning to address the challenges of incomplete alleviation of depression symptoms, attrition, and loss of follow-up in mental health treatment.
Three Interconnected Challenges
1. Incomplete Alleviation of Depression Symptoms
Current treatments — therapy and medication — work for many, but not completely for all. Studies show that nearly
50–60% of depression patients don't achieve full remission
after a first-line treatment. This happens because:
Treatment is often
one-size-fits-all
Symptom tracking between sessions is
inconsistent or absent
Patients suffer in silence between weekly or monthly appointments
There's a
significant gap
between clinic visits where symptoms can worsen undetected
2. Attrition — Dropping Out of Treatment
A large portion of patients
abandon treatment prematurely
, often before meaningful progress is made. Key reasons include:
Stigma
around seeking continued mental health support
Cost and accessibility
barriers — therapy is expensive and geographically limited
Lack of motivation
when progress feels slow or invisible
Feeling
disconnected
from a therapist they see infrequently
3. Loss to Follow-Up
Even patients who complete an initial treatment course often
disappear from care entirely
. This is dangerous because:
Depression is
episodic and recurring
— without follow-up, relapse goes undetected
There's no system proactively
reaching out
to at-risk patients
Clinicians are overwhelmed and cannot manually track hundreds of patients
The handoff between
crisis care and long-term care
is often broken
ABSTRACT
Neffi AI is a
Clinical Digital Therapeutics Platform
designed to improve digital mental healthcare by overcoming
the limitations of existing chatbots, which lack memory, personalization, and clinical safety.
The system uses
BERT-based emotion analysis
to classify user input into
96 clinically relevant emotional states
,
enabling deeper understanding beyond basic sentiment detection. It also incorporates a
vector-based memory system
to retain past conversations and build a continuous psychological profile of the user.
A
dynamic Mental Health Quotient (MHQ)
tracks user condition in real time, while a
predictive attrition algorithm
identifies early signs of disengagement and emotional decline. The platform integrates an
automation layer (n8n)
to
trigger real-time interventions such as alerts, reminders, and crisis responses.
Neffi AI provides both a
patient interface for interaction
and a
clinical dashboard for monitoring
, bridging the gap
between self-help tools and professional mental healthcare.

CONCLUSION
Neffi AI is not just a traditional chatbot but a clinically inspired digital mental health platform that combines artificial intelligence with proven psychological frameworks to deliver personalized and real-time support. By understanding 96+ emotional and depression states, integrating therapeutic response strategies like CBT and empathy-based interaction, and enabling automation through systems like n8n, the platform ensures both intelligent conversation and timely intervention. It addresses key limitations of existing solutions such as lack of memory, poor personalization, and absence of safety mechanisms. With features like MHQ scoring, attrition prediction, and
future integration with wearable devices, Neffi AI moves towards a proactive and continuous mental healthcare
model. Overall, it represents a shift from reactive chatbot systems to a smart, scalable, and clinically aware mental
health ecosystem capable of supporting users effectively and bridging the gap between technology and healthcare.
